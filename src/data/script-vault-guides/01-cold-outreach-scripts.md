# Cold Outreach Scripts

**20 word-for-word scripts for reaching out to people who don't know you yet. Customize the [BRACKETS] and send.**

> **Disclaimer:** These scripts are starting points you can adapt. Response rates depend on your industry, target, timing, and personalization. No specific outcome is guaranteed.

You don't need to send all 20 today. Pick the ones that match your situation.

**Time estimate:** 15 minutes to read, then customize and send as needed
**What you'll need:** Your target list, LinkedIn profile, and email access

---

## Cold Outreach Rules

Before you use any of these scripts, know the rules:

1. **Personalize every message.** Generic outreach gets ignored. Mention something specific about the person or company.
2. **Keep it short.** Under 100 words for initial outreach. Nobody reads a wall of text from a stranger.
3. **Ask for one thing.** Not a job. Not a referral. Not a 30-minute call. One small, specific ask.
4. **Follow up once.** If they don't respond after one follow-up, move on. Two messages total. Never three.
5. **Never lie.** Don't claim a mutual connection you don't have. Don't fabricate shared interests. People check.

## Cold Emails to Hiring Managers

### Script 1: The Direct Approach

```
Subject: [ROLE TITLE] at [COMPANY] — quick question

Hi [FIRST NAME],

I saw the [ROLE TITLE] posting on [WHERE YOU FOUND IT] and wanted
to reach out directly. I've spent [X YEARS] in [YOUR FIELD] and
most recently [ONE SENTENCE ABOUT RELEVANT EXPERIENCE].

I'd love to learn more about what the team is looking for. Would
you be open to a brief conversation?

Either way, I appreciate your time.

Best,
[YOUR NAME]
```

### Script 2: The Value-Lead

```
Subject: Idea for [COMPANY]'s [SPECIFIC CHALLENGE OR INITIATIVE]

Hi [FIRST NAME],

I've been following [COMPANY]'s work on [SPECIFIC PROJECT OR
INITIATIVE], and I noticed [SPECIFIC OBSERVATION OR OPPORTUNITY].

In my previous role at [COMPANY], I [SPECIFIC ACCOMPLISHMENT
RELATED TO THEIR CHALLENGE]. I'd love to share a couple of ideas
and learn more about how your team approaches this.

Would a 15-minute call work sometime this week or next?

Best,
[YOUR NAME]
```

### Script 3: The Mutual Connection (Only If True)

[YOUR NAME]

## LinkedIn Connection Requests

### Script 6: The Targeted Request

[INDUSTRY/FIELD]. I admire [COMPANY]'s work on [SPECIFIC THING]
and would love to connect and learn from your experience. Hope
to hear from you!

```
Hi [FIRST NAME], your recent post on [TOPIC] was spot-on,
especially [SPECIFIC POINT]. I work in [RELATED FIELD] and would
love to connect. Looking forward to more of your insights.

Here's a quick blurb you can forward:

"[YOUR NAME] is a [YOUR ROLE] with [X YEARS] of experience in
[FIELD]. They're interested in learning more about [COMPANY] and
any relevant opportunities. [YOUR NAME]'s email is [EMAIL]."

No pressure at all if it doesn't feel right. I appreciate you
either way.

Thanks,
[YOUR NAME]
```

and their time.

[YOUR NAME]

## Action After Sending

After sending any cold outreach:

1. **Log it in your tracker** with the date and a follow-up date (5-7 business days later)
2. **Don't check for a response obsessively.** Check once per day during your scheduled LinkedIn/email session.
3. **Send one follow-up** if no response in 5-7 business days (see the Follow-Up Scripts guide)
4. **Move on** if no response after the follow-up. Two messages total, then close it.

**Expect a 10-20% response rate on cold outreach.** That's normal and healthy. 80% silence is not rejection. It's the standard conversion rate.

## Key Takeaway

Cold outreach works when it's specific, brief, and personalized. Generic templates get ignored. These scripts give you the structure. The [BRACKETS] give you the customization points. Fill in the brackets with real details, send with confidence, and follow up once. That's the whole system.
