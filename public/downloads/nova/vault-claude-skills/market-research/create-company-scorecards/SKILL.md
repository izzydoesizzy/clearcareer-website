# Create Company Scorecards

Quickly do market research by creating company scorecards identifying key details.

**Best with:** Perplexity.ai

**How to use:**
1. Copy the Full Prompt below into Perplexity.ai 
2. Substitute `RED TEXT` with your own details
3. Copy/Paste your top achievements related to each priority below

---

## Prompt

```
I'm researching the field of FIELD

For each of the categories below, list the most popular and largest resources for its category.

Top 5 blogs/newsletters
Top 5 podcasts
Top 5 companies
Top 5 online communities
Top 5 influencers

Format each category as a table formatted like this:
NAME | DESCRIPTION | TYPE | URL
```
