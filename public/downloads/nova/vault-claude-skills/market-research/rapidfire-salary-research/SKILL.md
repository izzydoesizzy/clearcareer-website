# Rapidfire Salary Research

Instantly gather salary information based on your location and target company size

**Best with:** Perplexity.ai

**How to use:**
1. Copy the Full Prompt below into Perplexity.ai
2. Substitute `RED TEXT` with your own details

---

## Prompt

Step 1: Copy/Paste the prompt below into Perplexity, replacing the `RED TEXT` with your details

```
I'm considering a TARGET JOB TITLE role at COMPANY NAME in YOUR CITY.

Determine a research-backed salary range I should consider based on companies of a similar size, and similar roles in this location.
```

**Variables to replace:** TARGET JOB TITLE, YOUR CITY
