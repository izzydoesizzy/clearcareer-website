# Create a Quick Prospective Company List

Quickly generate a list of companies that do what you’re looking for.

**Best with:** Perplexity.ai

**How to use:**
1. Copy the Full Prompt below into Perplexity.ai
2. Substitute `RED TEXT` with your own details

---

## Prompt

Step 1: Copy/Paste the prompt below into Perplexity, replacing the `RED TEXT` with your details

```
Make a list of [companies/startups/tech companies] in [province/state/city] that focus on [topic, specific focus, specific industry].

Format the output as a 5-column table:
COMPANY NAME | DESCRIPTION | CITY | COMPANY URL
```

**Variables to replace:** [companies/startups/tech companies], [province/state/city], [topic, specific focus, specific industry]
