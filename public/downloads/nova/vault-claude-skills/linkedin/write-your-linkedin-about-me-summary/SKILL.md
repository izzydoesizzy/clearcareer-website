# Write Your LinkedIn “About Me” Summary

Write an effective “About Me” section for your LinkedIn profile

**Best with:** ChatGPT 3.5, ChatGPT 4.0

**How to use:**
1. Copy the Full Prompt below into ChatGPT
2. Substitute `RED TEXT` with your own details

---

## Prompt

```
Job Title: [YOUR JOB TITLE]
Industry: [YOUR INDUSTRY]

Act like a LinkedIn expert and career coach. 

Write a powerful "About Me" section to attract the the right companies and recruiters to my LinkedIn profile and contact me. 

Use the exact template below, including structure, spacing, and line breaks, but adapt the text to fit job title above or attached resume.

##Format:

#First section:
 Professional summary including Job Title, # of Years of Experience, 3 specific in-demand skills, specific industries, noteworthy companies or clients.

• • • • •

#Second section [5 Career Highlights]:
5 Career Highlights with the following structure:
[Emoji] [Achievement Title]:
[Quantified Achievement]

• • • • •

#What to include in the third section [Max 190 characters]: 
1-2 sentences describing specific types of roles and companies, their missions, and why they excite me.

#What to include in the fourth section [Max 130 characters]:
A specific call-to-action to incentivize recruiters to reach out to you.

#[Email Address]

##Sample Result (make sure to preserve the exact structure, specifically the line breaks): 

"Senior Project Manager with 20+ years of experience leveraging expertise in designing & optimizing processes in banking, manufacturing, and logistics. Masterminded $2M+ projects achieving six to seven figure gains and savings for industry giants like FedEx, Pella Corporation, ING, and Amerant Bank.

• • • • •

Career Highlights: 

💰 $850K Cost Savings - Amerant Bank 
Lowered operational costs $850K including negotiating 39% price decrease in bankcard manufacturing and 57% reduction in shipping rates.

⏱️ 88% Processing Time Decrease - Amerant Bank 
Achieved 88% processing time reduction in the debit card instant issuance project: Improved efficiency and enhanced client experience.

💲 5% Profit Margins Increase - Pella Corporation
Increased installation profit margins 5% without raising prices. Increased daily installations 30% by organizing specialized crews and optimizing scheduling.

📦 50% On-Hand Inventory Reduction - Pella Corporation
Accomplished a 50% reduction of on-hand inventory and associated carrying costs by successfully implementing just-in-time ordering best practices.

🛒 35% Sales Booking Cycle Reduction - Pella Corporation
Obtained 35% reduction in the sales booking cycle by training salespersons on install openings’ measurements.

• • • • •

I get excited about leading enterprise-level projects that leave a lasting, positive impact, at companies that value corporate social responsibility, customer centricity and diversity.

Looking for your next Project Manager? Let's discuss how to streamline the bottlenecks that are stunting your company’s growth!

Connect with me at: → example@example.com"
```

**Variables to replace:** [YOUR JOB TITLE], [YOUR INDUSTRY], [5 Career Highlights], [Emoji], [Achievement Title], [Quantified Achievement], [Max 190 characters], [Max 130 characters], [Email Address], YOUR JOB TITLE, YOUR INDUSTRY
