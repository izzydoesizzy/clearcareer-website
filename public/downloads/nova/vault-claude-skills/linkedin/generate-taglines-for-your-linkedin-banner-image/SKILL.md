# Generate Taglines For Your LinkedIn Banner Image

Generate a list of industry specific value-propositions to intrigue employers visiting your linkedin.

**Best with:** ChatGPT 3.5, ChatGPT 4.0

**How to use:**
1. Copy the Full Prompt below into ChatGPT
2. Substitute `RED TEXT` with your own details
3. Follow up prompt to produce more specific results

---

## Prompt

Step 1: Copy/Paste the prompt below into ChatGPT, replacing the `RED TEXT` with your details.

```
[Job Title] = YOUR JOB TITLE
[Field/Industry] = YOUR FIELD/INDUSTRY

Here are a number of job-seeker slogans.  Write a list of 20 more slogans like this that a job seeker can use to stand out and attract employers to interview them. 

Each slogan must present a specific skill or value proposition that will be of interest to potential employers. 

Syntax Perfected, User Experience Elevated
The Risk Mitigator: Internal Controls for Secure Financials
Front-end Magic, Back-end Brilliance
Fort Knox for Web Apps: Top-Tier Security Consulting
Breaking Barriers, Not Websites
Bulletproof Web Apps Through Automated Testing
Turning User Data into Actionable Insights
Transforming Financial Data into Strategic Assets
Tax Planning that Turns Savings into Investments
Simplifying Mergers and Acquisitions: Due Diligence Done Right

# Output: 
- Format the output as a 2-column table. | Description | Tagline |
```

**Variables to replace:** [Job Title], [Field/Industry], YOUR JOB TITLE, YOUR FIELD/INDUSTRY
