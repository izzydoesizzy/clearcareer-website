# Write Discoverable LinkedIn Headlines

Generate LinkedIn headlines that make you more searchable and increase profile visits

**Best with:** ChatGPT 3.5, ChatGPT 4.0

**How to use:**
1. Copy the Full Prompt below into ChatGPT
2. Substitute `RED TEXT` with your own details

---

## Prompt

```
Job Title = YOUR JOB TITLE
Industry = YOUR INDUSTRY

**Objective:** Write 10 high-impact LinkedIn headlines for a [Job Title] in the [Industry] industry in the structure and style of the examples below.  

These headlines should make the profile irresistible to recruiters by focusing on impact, value proposition, and high-value quantified achievements.

Output the results as a 2-column table that follows the structure and style of the examples below, only replaced with the new details you have.

**Sample Results & Output:**
| # | LinkedIn Headline |
|---|---|
| 1 | Sales Planning Analyst \| 8+ Years in CPG & Commodities \| Portfolio of $40M+ Major Canadian Retailers including Walmart, Loblaws, Metro, and Sobeys \| Saved $600K+ by Streamlining Client Promotions |
| 2 | Senior Accounts Receivable Manager \| Portfolio of $1Billion+ Including Fortune 500 Companies such as PepsiCo, Walmart, Costco, ExxonMobil, and Coca Cola \| Recovered 40% of portfolio past due balance $56M in under 9mo |
| 3 | Strategic Program Manager in Technology and Manufacturing \| SAP \| Oracle \| Power BI \| Saving 60 Hours / Week Through Automations Macros and Streamlined Operations \| Retained a $20M Project from Churn |
| 4 | Marketing Director \| 15+ Years in B2B and B2C \| Led Campaigns for Brands like Nike, Apple, and Samsung \| Drove 120% Increase in Online Engagement in 6 Months |
| 5 | Financial Analyst \| Expertise in Financial Modeling and Forecasting \| Managed Portfolios Worth $500M+ Including Clients like JP Morgan and Goldman Sachs \| Reduced Operational Costs by 15% |

(continue until 10)
```

**Variables to replace:** [Job Title], [Industry], YOUR JOB TITLE, YOUR INDUSTRY
