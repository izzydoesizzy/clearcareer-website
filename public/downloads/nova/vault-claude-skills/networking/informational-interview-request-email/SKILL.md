# Informational Interview Request Email

Write an compelling and impressive email to land an informational interview

**Best with:** ChatGPT 4.0

**How to use:**
1. Copy the Full Prompt below into ChatGPT
2. Substitute `RED TEXT` with your own details

---

## Prompt

```
Your Name = YOUR NAME
Job Title = YOUR JOB TITLE
Field/Industry = YOUR INDUSTRY
Years of Experience = # OF YEARS OF EXPERIENCE
Target Company = NAME OF COMPANY
Contact Name = NAME OF CONTACT

# Context - How I came across this contact:
INSERT HOW YOU FOUND THEM (BLOG POST, INTERVIEW, YOUTUBE VIDEO, PODCAST, RESOURCE, TUTORIAL ETC)

# Context - What stood out about what I read/saw: 
MENTION SOMETHING THAT GENUINELY STOOD OUT TO YOU ABOUT THEM (Philosophy, opinion, resource etc)

# What are you hoping to achieve in your career?
INSERT YOUR CAREER MISSION/PHILOSOPHY/ASPIRATION

# Quantified Achievement
INSERT IMPRESSIVE QUANTIFIED ACHIEVEMENT

# Write an email to [Contact] at [Target Company] in [Field/Industry]. Mention how I came across their profile, something specific I admired or found interesting.  

Use the template below and format as markdown. Keep all paragraphs below 200 characters.

"Hi [FIRST NAME],

I recently came across your [Podcast, Blog Post, Video, Tutorial, Interview etc] and [something specific and inspirational or insightful from what you read]. What you said about [topic] really resonated with me and made me think of [personal achievement, mission, inspiration, methodology].

A bit about me: 
My name is [YOUR NAME], and I’m a [YOUR JOB TITLE] working in [YOUR INDUSTRY]. 

- [Professional Quantified Achievement]
- [Focus and Professional Mission]
- [Work you’re looking to do more of]

Given your extensive experience in the field and your inspiring work at [Company Name], I’d love your advice on how to approach breaking into a role in [Industry / Type of Company].

Would you be open for a short 20 minute call on [SPECIFIC DATE/TIME]?

It would be immensely impactful for me. 

Looking forward to hearing from you!

All the best,
[YOUR NAME]
LinkedIn Profile URL"
```

**Variables to replace:** [Contact], [Target Company], [Field/Industry], [FIRST NAME], [Podcast, Blog Post, Video, Tutorial, Interview etc], [something specific and inspirational or insightful from what you read], [topic], [personal achievement, mission, inspiration, methodology], [YOUR NAME], [YOUR JOB TITLE], [YOUR INDUSTRY], [Professional Quantified Achievement], [Focus and Professional Mission], [Work you’re looking to do more of], [Company Name], [Industry / Type of Company], [SPECIFIC DATE/TIME], YOUR NAME, YOUR JOB TITLE, YOUR INDUSTRY, YOUR CAREER MISSION/PHILOSOPHY/ASPIRATION
