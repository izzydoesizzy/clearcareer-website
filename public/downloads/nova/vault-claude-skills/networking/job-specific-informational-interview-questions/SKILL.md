# Job-Specific Informational Interview Questions

Write a list of industry and job specific questions to ask in your next informational interview.

**Best with:** ChatGPT 3.5, ChatGPT 4.0

**How to use:**
1. Copy the Full Prompt below into ChatGPT
2. Substitute `RED TEXT` with your own details

---

## Prompt

```
Desired Job Title: JOB TITLE
Desired Field: FIELD/INDUSTRY

Act like an interview coach.

I'm a job-seeker looking to engage in informational interviews.

Rewrite the questions below to cater specifically to my situation.

Output the result as a table like the one below: 

Category | Keywords | Question

| Category                   | Keywords                        | Question                                                  |
|-----------------------------|---------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| Introduction to Role       | Transition, Background           | How did you transition into Technical Product Management, and what drew you to this role? |
| Role Clarity               | Responsibilities                | Can you describe the primary responsibilities of your role as a Technical Product Manager? |
| Role Comparison            | Differentiation, Skills          | How does your role differ from that of non-technical Product Managers in your organization? |
| Daily Workflow             | Routine, Activities              | What does a typical day look like for you? |
| Daily Workflow             | Time Management                 | How much of your time is spent on technical tasks versus business tasks? |
| Collaboration              | Teamwork, Stakeholders           | How do you collaborate with engineering teams and other stakeholders? |
| Product Development        | Ideation, Launch                | Can you take me through the process of developing a new feature from ideation to launch? |
| Challenges                 | Problem-Solving, Experience      | Can you share an experience where you had to overcome a significant challenge in a product lifecycle? |
| Skills and Development     | Skills, Growth                  | What technical skills are indispensable for your daily work? |
| Skills and Development     | Soft Skills, Effectiveness      | What business or soft skills do you rely on most? |
| Strategic Planning         | Roadmaps, Strategies            | What best practices do you follow when defining product roadmaps and strategies? |
| Industry Engagement        | Trends, Adaptation              | How do you stay informed about industry trends and incorporate them into your product planning? |
| Innovation and Feedback    | Innovation, User Feedback       | How does your company approach product innovation and user feedback? |
| Resource Discovery         | Learning, Resources              | Can you recommend specific events, resources, blogs, communities, or newsletters about industry trends and best practices? |
| Career Pathway             | Entry, Strategy, Advice         | What steps would you take in my place to break into a Technical Product Manager role? |
| Company Exploration        | Opportunities, Culture           | What companies come to mind that you'd recommend I consider exploring for opportunities in Technical Product Management? |
| Interview Preparation      | Fit, Evaluation, Process        | What are the most critical questions or interview processes you would use to assess if a candidate is a good fit for a PM role? |
| Personal Insights          | Advice, Reflection              | What is one piece of advice you wish you had received when you started out as a Technical Product Manager? |
| Reflection and Advice      | Lessons Learned, Knowledge      | Is there anything you wish you knew before you entered this role? |
| Networking and Resources   | Contacts, Resources             | Are there any other people you recommend I speak with or resources I should look into? |
```
