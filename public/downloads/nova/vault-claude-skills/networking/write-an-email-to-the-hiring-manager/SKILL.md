# Write an Email to the Hiring Manager

Write an email to the hiring manager about a job

**Best with:** ChatGPT 3.5

**How to use:**
1. Copy/Paste the full job posting into ChatGPT
2. Copy/Paste your resume into ChatGPT
3. Copy the Full Prompt below into ChatGPT
4. Substitute `RED TEXT` with your own details

---

## Step 1

Step 1: Copy/Paste this prompt followed by the job description into ChatGPT then hit enter.

```
Here is a job posting I am interested in. Reply by saying "Ok, I have received this job posting for future reference" if you have received this information.

[COPY/PASTE FULL JOB DESCRIPTION]
```

**Variables to replace:** [COPY/PASTE FULL JOB DESCRIPTION]

## Step 2

Step 2: Copy/Paste this prompt followed by your resume into ChatGPT then hit enter.

```
Here is a copy of my resume. Reply with a one word comment saying "Ok, I have received your resume for future reference" if you have received this information.

[COPY/PASTE YOUR RESUME TEXT]
```

**Variables to replace:** [COPY/PASTE YOUR RESUME TEXT], YOUR RESUME TEXT

## Step 3

Step 3: Copy/Paste this prompt and your resume into ChatGPT

```
[Hiring Manager Name] = HIRING MANAGER NAME
[Your Full Name] = YOUR FULL NAME
[Company] = TARGET COMPANY NAME

Using my resume and the job description above, write an to the hiring manager of[Company] based on the 3 main priorities from the job description and outline how I am a good fit using my relevant achievements.

Use the template and format below and output as markdown.

"Hi [Hiring Manager Name],

My name is [YOUR FULL NAME] and I am writing to connect regarding the [JOB TITLE] role at [COMPANY NAME].

It sounds like the [TEAM/DEPARTMENT] team at [COMPANY] is looking for someone with [PRIORITY 1], [PRIORITY 2], and [PRIORITY 3].

Let me share how my experience aligns with these requirements:

**1. [PRIORITY 1]:** [TOP ACHIEVEMENT RELATED TO PRIORITY 1]

**2. [PRIORITY 1]:** [TOP ACHIEVEMENT RELATED TO PRIORITY 2]

**3. [PRIORITY 3]:** [TOP ACHIEVEMENT RELATED TO PRIORITY 3]

I’m confident that I’d make a stellar addition to the team and would love to chat more! Would you be open to a short 20-minute call on [SPECIFIC DATE AND TIME] to learn more about the role and the team?

Thanks in advance for your consideration! Looking forward to hearing from you!

All the best,

[YOUR FULL NAME]
[LINKEDIN URL]
[PHONE NUMBER]"

Example output: 

"Hi Jeff,

My name is James Smith and I am writing to express my interest in the Demand Planner role at McCain Foods.

It sounds like the team at McCain Foods is looking for someone who can efficiently create and maintain a demand plan, incorporate key business intelligence into the demand signal, and lead a dialogue each month for their respective customers and products with key counterparts in Marketing and Sales.

Let me share how my experience aligns with these requirements:

**1. Efficient Creation and Maintenance of a Demand Plan:** I saved Advantage Solutions $50-100K per month by identifying and eliminating redundant or overlapping promotions through daily sales forecasting monitoring for 40+ SKUs.

**2. Incorporating Key Business Intelligence into the Demand Signal:** I saved Freshii $10K per month by optimizing the replenishment process with the warehousing and packaging teams, which reduced overstock instances by 60%.

**3. Leading Dialogue for Respective Customers and Products:** I managed a portfolio of $40M+ for major Canadian retailers including Walmart, Loblaws, Metro, and Sobeys. This involved constant communication and coordination with these key customers to manage their demands and expectations effectively.

I’m confident that I’d make a stellar addition to the McCain Foods team and would love to chat more! Would you be open to a short 20-minute call on Friday at 10:00am to learn more about the role and the team?

Thanks in advance for your consideration! Looking forward to hearing from you!

All the best,

James Smith
linkedin.com/in/jamessmith
647-000-0000"
```

**Variables to replace:** [Hiring Manager Name], [Your Full Name], [Company], [YOUR FULL NAME], [JOB TITLE], [COMPANY NAME], [TEAM/DEPARTMENT], [COMPANY], [PRIORITY 1], [PRIORITY 2], [PRIORITY 3], [TOP ACHIEVEMENT RELATED TO PRIORITY 1], [TOP ACHIEVEMENT RELATED TO PRIORITY 2], [TOP ACHIEVEMENT RELATED TO PRIORITY 3], [SPECIFIC DATE AND TIME], [LINKEDIN URL], [PHONE NUMBER], YOUR FULL NAME, TARGET COMPANY NAME
