# Write a Post-Interview Thank You Email

This is your “Silver Bullet”. An unforgettable thank you email that will get you hired

**Best with:** ChatGPT 4.0

**How to use:**
1. Copy the Full Prompt below into ChatGPT
2. Substitute `RED TEXT` with your own details
3. Delete the `OPTIONAL PURPLE SECTION` if desired
4. Copy/Paste your top achievements related to each priority below

---

## Prompt

```
Job Title = TARGET JOB TITLE
Target Industry = TARGET INDUSTRY NAME
Interviewer Name(s) = INTERVIEWER NAME(S) 
Company Name = INTERVIEWING COMPANY NAME 

Write a post-interview thank you email for the interviewers I spoke with at [COMPANY NAME] for the [JOB TITLE]].

# Interview Context 

# What I liked about the interview, people, and company:
NAME SOMETHING YOU LIKED ABOUT THE INTERVIEW, TEAM, OR COMPANY 

# Top 3 company priorities for this role based on the conversation and what I know about the job:
[Priority 1]: NUMBER 1 PRIORITY FOR THE ROLE
[Priority 2]: NUMBER 2 PRIORITY FOR THE ROLE
[Priority 3]: NUMBER 3 PRIORITY FOR THE ROLE

# Here are my relevant achievements for each of those 3 priorities:
[Priority 1 Achievement]: INSERT RELEVANT ACHIEVEMENT TO PRIORITY 1
[Priority 2 Achievement]: INSERT RELEVANT ACHIEVEMENT TO PRIORITY 2
[Priority 3 Achievement]: INSERT RELEVANT ACHIEVEMENT TO PRIORITY 3

**# [OPTIONAL SECTION] (Remove if desired)**
What is a mistake you made or concern they seem to have about you:
INSERT MISTAKE YOU MADE OR CONCERN ABOUT YOUR EXPERIENCE

What relevant experience (direct or indirect) do you have that relieves this concern?
INSERT RELEVANT EXPERIENCE

# Below is the template for the email.
# Format the email in markdown.

"Hi [First Name],

Thank you so much for taking the time to speak with me today. It was such a pleasure meeting you all and learning about the company and the team. [Memorable moment from the interview / something you learned and liked/appreciated].
 
Based on our conversation, it sounds like the team at [Company Name] is looking for someone who can [Priority 1], [Priority 2], [Priority 3].
 
Based on that, here are a few additional things I’m confident will make me the ideal fit for the role:

**1. [Priority 1]**
[Relevant achievement that showcases your proficiency, approach, and competency]

**2. [Priority 2]**
[Relevant achievement that showcases your proficiency, approach, and competency]

**3. [Priority 3]**
[Relevant achievement that showcases your proficiency, approach, and competency]

[Optional section: Address a concern, or a mistake you made]

I’m excited about the opportunity to work with the team at [Company Name] because of [Challenge/Opportunity/Value]. [Restate your fit, and value proposition].

Thanks again for the opportunity, and I look forward to hearing from you.

All the best,
[Your Name]"
```

**Variables to replace:** [COMPANY NAME], [JOB TITLE], [Priority 1], [Priority 2], [Priority 3], [Priority 1 Achievement], [Priority 2 Achievement], [Priority 3 Achievement], [OPTIONAL SECTION], [First Name], [Memorable moment from the interview / something you learned and liked/appreciated], [Company Name], [Relevant achievement that showcases your proficiency, approach, and competency], [Optional section: Address a concern, or a mistake you made], [Challenge/Opportunity/Value], [Restate your fit, and value proposition], [Your Name], TARGET JOB TITLE, TARGET INDUSTRY NAME, YOUR EXPERIENCE
