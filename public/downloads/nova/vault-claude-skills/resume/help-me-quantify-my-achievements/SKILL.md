# Help me Quantify My Achievements

Ask a series of questions about my resume to help me quantify achievements

**Best with:** ChatGPT 3.5, ChatGPT 4.0

**How to use:**
1. Copy the Full Prompt below into ChatGPT
2. Substitute `RED TEXT` with your own details

---

## Prompt

```
**Prompt for ChatGPT**:  

"Using the attached resume, generate 4-5 questions for each bullet point in the resume to help the individual identify and quantify the impact of their achievements. Focus on questions that lead them to uncover specific metrics or data. When they don’t have immediate access to data, suggest targeted questions to help them estimate reasonable ranges for those metrics. For each bullet point, include the original achievement verbatim before listing the questions. Your goal is to help them craft more quantified and impactful achievements."

**Example Output**:

---

**Problem-Solving & Technical Expertise**  
**Achievement:**  
"Utilized strong analytical skills to break down complex problems and develop data-driven solutions for critical system issues. Successfully leveraged advanced ERP systems knowledge (SAP, Infor M3) and MS Office tools for impactful results."

- How many critical system issues did you resolve, and what was the average time taken to resolve them before and after your intervention?
- What was the percentage reduction in system downtime or errors as a result of your solutions?
- How many users or departments were impacted by your ERP system improvements?
- Can you quantify the financial or time-saving benefits from your data-driven solutions, such as reduced manual work or error correction?

---

Ensure that every section in the resume follows this structure, providing detailed questions to prompt the individual to quantify their results effectively.

```
