# Write a Professional Summary

Write 10 sample high-quality professional summaries tailored to your job title and industry

**Best with:** ChatGPT 3.5, ChatGPT 4.0

**How to use:**
1. Copy the Full Prompt below into ChatGPT
2. Substitute `RED TEXT` with your own details

---

## Prompt

```
[Job Title] =  CURRENT JOB TITLE
[Industry] = CURRENT INDUSTRY
[Company] = YOUR LAST/CURRENT COMPANY
[Experience] = # OF YEARS OF EXPERIENCE

Act like a resume-writing expert. 

Using the context above, write 10 professional summaries using the structure from the examples below.

# What to Include
What to include in every Professional Summary:
- Job Title and Seniority
- Years of Experience
- 2-3 Job-Specific Skills
- Industry
- Powerful Quantified Achievement 
- Recognizable major client names (Example: Coca Cola, Shell, McDonalds)
- Portfolio size (Example: $50 Billion).

# Template:
Follow the template below and examples at the end to write the professional summaries.
Template: [Title] with [Years of Experience]+ years of experience in [3 Skills] in the [Industry] industry. [Major Quantified Achievement].  Portfolio of [Portfolio Size] including clients such as [Noteworthy Clients]."

# Powerful Professional Summary Examples and sample output:
1. **$1 Billion+ Portfolio**: Accounts Receivable Manager with 15+ years of experience working in CPG and Manufacturing. 7+ Years of Experience in Credit, Collections and Accounts Receivable. Managed a portfolio of $1Billion+ including Fortune 500 companies such as PepsiCo, Walmart, Costco, ExxonMobil, and CocaCola.
2. **Saved Retailers $600K**: Demand Planning Analyst with 8+ years of experience in forecasting, sales planning, and inventory management in the Consumer Packaged Goods and Commodities industries. Portfolio of $40M+ major Canadian retailers including Walmart, Loblaws, Metro, and Sobeys. Saved clients over $600K by identifying and streamlining client promotions.
3. **20 Years in Valuation**: Risk Management and Mergers and Acquisitions Specialist with over 20 years working in valuation, finance, risk and wealth management, and M&A. Worked with 40+ companies in Risk Management and on 30+ Mergers and Acquisitions, 5 of which were closed at over $1 billion USD.
4. **Top Accuracy Performer**: Sr Demand Planner with 4 years of experience managing forecast and retail analysis for US emerging markets (around $85MM & 1,000+ SKUs) and US ecommerce major accounts (around $120MM and 2,000+ SKUs). Top forecast accuracy performer (over 70% average) promoted 3 times in the past 4 years.
5. **Cost Saving Program Manager**: Program Manager with 10 years of experience in Technology and Manufacturing. Strong expertise in strategic planning, resource allocation, and stakeholder management. Led cross-functional teams of more than 30 individuals to successfully deliver multi-million dollar projects valued up to $50 million, driving operational efficiency and cost savings for companies such as IBM, Sanmina, and Dynapro Pumps.
```

**Variables to replace:** [Job Title], [Industry], [Company], [Experience], [Title], [Years of Experience], [3 Skills], [Major Quantified Achievement], [Portfolio Size], [Noteworthy Clients], YOUR LAST/CURRENT COMPANY
