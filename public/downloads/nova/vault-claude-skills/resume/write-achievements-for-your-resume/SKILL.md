# Write Achievements for Your Resume

Jog your memory by generating a list of achievements based on your job title and industry

**Best with:** ChatGPT 3.5, ChatGPT 4.0

**How to use:**
1. Copy the Full Prompt below into ChatGPT
2. Substitute `RED TEXT` with your own details
3. Follow up prompt to produce more specific results

---

## Step 1

Step 1: Copy/Paste the prompt below into ChatGPT, replacing the `RED TEXT` with your details.

```
[Job Title] = YOUR JOB TITLE
[Field/Industry] = YOUR FIELD/INDUSTRY

# Task: 
Write 20 sample quantified achievements for the resume of a [Job Title] in the [Field/Industry] Field/Industry.

# Output: 
- Format the output as a 2-column table.
- Use the format and structure from the quantified achievements below and write new achievements using the Job Title and Industry above.

| Description | Sample Quantified Achievement |
|---|---|
| Social Media Reach | Grew social media following by 40% in 4 weeks, from 50,000 to 70,000 followers, increasing the audience for content distribution. |
| Collaborations | Collaborated with 10 influential creators, resulting in a 20% increase in subscribers/followers, (10,000 new subscribers) and reaching new target audiences. |
| Product Sales | Increased sales of bakery items by 25%, from 80 to 100 items sold per day, enhancing overall shop revenue. |
| Sales Targets | Exceeded individual sales targets by 20% in 2022 Q4, resulting in $3 million in net profit |
| Phone Orders | Process 20+ phone orders per day, generating an average of $300 in revenue per call |
| Sales Record | Set the company record for most add-ons sold in a single month (51, totaling $3,500) |
| Training | Trained 20+ new employees in phone etiquette, and sales and conflict resolution techniques |
| Online Store | Created an online store as part of a group project that generated $5,000 in T-shirt sales for a local charity |
| Head Waiter | Served as head waiter on a staff of 14 waiters and bussers |
| Event Planning | Planned and executed 25+ corporate events in 2023, with an average attendance of 100+ guests per event. |
| Cost Reduction | Managed event budgets totalling over $500,000 in 2023, achieving an average of 10% cost savings per event, resulting in a total cost reduction of $50,000 and optimizing resources. |
| Portfolio Growth | Grew client portfolio value by 15% in a year, increasing from $2 million to $2.3 million, enhancing client wealth. |
| Check Ins | Managed the front desk, checking 45+ guests in and out of their rooms and confirming 20+ new reservations via phone and email each shift |
| Transactions | - Processed an average of $5,000 in cash and credit transactions per shift |
| Conversion Rate | Revamped the website's landing page, increasing the conversion rate from 2% (200 out of 10,000 visitors) to 4% (400 out of 10,000 visitors). |
| Cost Savings | Saved the company $40,000 per year by negotiating new vendor contracts |

# Anytime percentage change is mentioned, indicate the raw number to illustrate impact:
Example: Reduced farming operational costs by 10%, from $1 million to $900,000, through efficient resource use, improving overall profit margins.
```

**Variables to replace:** [Job Title], [Field/Industry], YOUR JOB TITLE, YOUR FIELD/INDUSTRY

## Step 2

Step 2: Copy/Paste this follow up prompt below to generate more results closer to what you want. Substitute the `RED TEXT` with some of your own projects and responsibilities:

```
Now write 20 more achievements like this, providing suggestions for quantified achievements based on some of my projects and responsibilities below:

Responsibilities:
- [Responsibility 1]
- [Responsibility 2]
- [Project 1]
- [Project 2]
```

**Variables to replace:** [Responsibility 1], [Responsibility 2], [Project 1], [Project 2]
