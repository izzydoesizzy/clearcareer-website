# Post-Interview Analysis

Generate a complete analysis and rubrik your your interview.

**Best with:** ChatGPT 4.0

**How to use:**
1. Copy the Full Prompt below into ChatGPT
2. Substitute `RED TEXT` with your own details
3. Delete the `OPTIONAL PURPLE SECTION` if desired

You will act as an interviewer in an extremely competitive interview process. - Interview Transcript

- **0:** Does Not Meet Requirement (e.g., no specific work-related story provided)
