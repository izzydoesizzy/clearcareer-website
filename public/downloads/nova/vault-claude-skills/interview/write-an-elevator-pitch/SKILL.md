# Write an Elevator Pitch

Write variations of an elevator pitch based on your resume.

**Best with:** ChatGPT 4.0

**How to use:**
1. Copy the Full Prompt below into ChatGPT
2. Substitute `RED TEXT` with your own details
3. Delete the `OPTIONAL PURPLE SECTION` if desired
4. Copy/Paste your top achievements related to each priority below

---

## Prompt

```
I’d like you to act as a career coach and communication coach. I will paste in a resume below. Based on that resume, generate 5 different elevator pitch variations using the following formats:

---

Template 1: Quick & Clear  
Use this structure:
- Name  
- Current or most recent role or professional identity  
- Key skills or specializations  
- A clear achievement or experience highlight  
- What role or opportunity they're currently seeking  

---

Template 2: Impact-Oriented  
Use this structure:
- Name and role/industry  
- One or two strong, quantified achievements  
- Area of expertise  
- What role or opportunity they’re targeting now  

Please include a mix of roles that the person may be qualified for based on their resume—such as Developer Advocate, Project Manager, Technical Writer, Employee Experience Specialist, or Product Manager—and tailor each pitch slightly to align with that goal. Make sure each pitch is 30–45 seconds long when spoken aloud.

---

**Examples**:

🎯 1. The “Quick & Clear” Pitch  
Perfect for introverts or high-traffic events  

Template:  
"Hi, I’m [Name]. I’m a [Job Title] with experience in [Industry or Key Skill Area], especially strong in [Top Skills]. I’m currently looking for [Target Role or Company Type], ideally where I can [What You Love Doing or Your Value Add]."

Example:  
"Hi, I’m Jordan. I’m a marketing strategist with 5+ years of experience in B2B tech. I specialize in lead gen and campaign performance, and I’m currently looking for a growth marketing role at a startup that values data and creativity."

🌟 2. The “Impact-Oriented” Pitch  
Great when you have strong metrics or want to stand out  

Template:  
"I'm [Name], a [Job Title or Field] who recently [Quantified Achievement or Project]. I specialize in [Key Skills], and I’m looking for opportunities in [Industry or Role] where I can drive similar results."

Example:  
"I'm Aisha, a customer success manager who helped reduce churn by 20% in 6 months through a new onboarding strategy. I love building strong client relationships and I’m looking for a role at a mission-driven SaaS company."

---

Once you're ready, I’ll paste the resume below.

```

**Variables to replace:** [Name], [Job Title], [Industry or Key Skill Area], [Top Skills], [Target Role or Company Type], [What You Love Doing or Your Value Add], [Job Title or Field], [Quantified Achievement or Project], [Key Skills], [Industry or Role]
