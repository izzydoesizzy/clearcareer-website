# Generate “Tell Me About Yourself”

Generate a precise, targeted answer to the common interview question

**Best with:** ChatGPT 4.0

**How to use:**
1. Copy the Full Prompt below into ChatGPT
2. Substitute `RED TEXT` with your own details
3. Delete the `OPTIONAL PURPLE SECTION` if desired
4. Copy/Paste your top achievements related to each priority below

---

## Prompt

```
As a career coaching assistant, help the job seeker craft a compelling and personalized answer to the interview question “Tell Me About Yourself.” Use their resume and the job description to highlight their most relevant skills and experiences. Follow the template below to structure the answer.

Inputs: (use a made up resume and job description for the time being)

Template:

	1.	Introduction:
	•	State your name and professional title.
	•	Mention your current or most recent role and company.
	2.	Most Recent Professional Background:
	•	Highlight most recent relevant professional experience 
	•	Highlight a significant quantified achievement with metrics, very specific actions taken and impressive results to showcase your value. 
	3.	Alignment with the Role:
	•	Identify the top 2-3 requirements from the job description.
	•	Explain how your skills and experiences align with these needs, providing specific examples.
	4.	Unique Value Proposition:
	•	Share what sets you apart, such as unique skills or perspectives.
	•	Mention any personal traits that make you a good cultural fit.
	5.	Motivation, goals and conclusion.
	•	Explain why you’re interested in this role and company.
	•	Discuss how the position aligns with your aspirarions and qualifications 
	•	Express enthusiasm for the opportunity.
```
