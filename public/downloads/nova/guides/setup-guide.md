# How to Set Up Nova

Nova runs inside Claude, Anthropic's AI assistant. You'll create a "Project" that loads all of Nova's career frameworks, so every conversation you have is powered by the same strategies used with 300+ coaching clients.

Total setup time: about 5 minutes.

---

## Step 1: Create a Claude Account

1. Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) and sign up for a free account
2. You can use Google, email, or Apple to sign up
3. The free tier works. You don't need to pay for Claude Pro (though it gives you more messages per day)

## Step 2: Create a New Project

1. On the left sidebar, click **"Projects"**
2. Click **"Create Project"**
3. Name it **"Nova"** (or whatever you'd like)
4. In the **"Custom Instructions"** box, paste the contents of the file called `nova-project-instructions.md` that came with your purchase. This tells Claude how to act as your career strategist.

## Step 3: Upload the Prompt Frameworks

1. In your Nova project, click **"Add Project Knowledge"**
2. Upload all 6 prompt files from the `prompts/` folder:
   - `01-resume-skills.md`
   - `02-linkedin.md`
   - `03-interview.md`
   - `04-networking-outreach.md`
   - `05-market-research.md`
   - `06-negotiation-lifecycle.md`
3. These files contain 50 career frameworks that Nova will reference when helping you

## Step 4: Upload Your Resume

1. Still in Project Knowledge, click **"Add"** again
2. Upload your current resume (PDF, DOCX, or TXT)
3. This lets Nova reference your actual experience when generating content

That's it. You're ready.

---

## How to Use Nova

1. Open your Nova project
2. Start a new conversation
3. Tell Nova what you need. For example:
   - "Help me write a professional summary for a product manager role"
   - "Write 3 LinkedIn headline options for me"
   - "I have an interview at Google next week. Help me prepare."
   - "Research salary ranges for a Senior Data Analyst in Toronto"
   - "Write a cold email to a hiring manager at Shopify"

Nova will use the frameworks and your resume to generate personalized, ready-to-use content.

## Tips

- **Be specific.** "Write me a cover letter" is fine. "Write me a cover letter for a Senior PM role at Stripe, emphasizing my experience scaling B2B products" is better.
- **One task per conversation.** Start a new chat for each deliverable (resume, LinkedIn, interview prep). This keeps Nova focused.
- **Iterate.** If the first output isn't quite right, tell Nova what to change. "Make the tone more confident" or "Add more metrics to bullet #3."
- **Upload job descriptions.** When preparing for a specific role, paste the job description into the chat. Nova will tailor everything to match.

## Troubleshooting

**"I hit my message limit"**
The free Claude tier has a daily message limit. You can either wait until tomorrow or upgrade to Claude Pro for more messages. Either way, your Nova project and all uploaded files will be waiting for you.

**"Nova isn't using my resume"**
Make sure your resume is uploaded as Project Knowledge (not just pasted into a chat). Project Knowledge persists across all conversations in the project.

**"The output doesn't match my industry"**
Be more specific in your request. Include your industry, target role, years of experience, and any specific companies you're targeting. The more context Nova has, the better the output.
