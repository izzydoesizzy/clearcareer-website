# Your First 30 Minutes with Nova

You've set up your Nova project. Now let's get some quick wins. Follow these 4 steps in order. Each one builds on the last.

---

## Win #1: Professional Summary (5 minutes)

Your professional summary is the first thing recruiters read. Let's nail it.

**Say this to Nova:**

> "Using my resume, write me a professional summary for a [TARGET ROLE] position. Keep it to 3-4 sentences. Lead with my strongest qualification."

**What you'll get:** A concise, punchy summary you can drop into your resume and LinkedIn profile right now.

---

## Win #2: LinkedIn Headlines (5 minutes)

Most people waste their LinkedIn headline on their current job title. Your headline is searchable real estate.

**Say this to Nova:**

> "Write me 5 LinkedIn headline options. I want to be found by recruiters searching for [TARGET ROLE] roles. Include keywords, results, and what makes me different."

**What you'll get:** 5 headline options ranging from keyword-heavy (for search) to personality-driven (for standing out). Pick the one that feels most like you.

---

## Win #3: Salary Research (10 minutes)

Before any conversation about money, you need to know your numbers.

**Say this to Nova:**

> "Research salary ranges for a [TARGET ROLE] in [YOUR CITY]. I have [X] years of experience. Give me a low, mid, and high range with sources."

**What you'll get:** A research-backed salary range so you never walk into a negotiation blind.

---

## Win #4: Your "Tell Me About Yourself" Answer (10 minutes)

This is the most common interview question and most people ramble through it.

**Say this to Nova:**

> "Help me write a 'Tell Me About Yourself' answer for a [TARGET ROLE] at [COMPANY]. Use the 5-part framework from your interview prep knowledge. Reference my resume for specifics."

**What you'll get:** A structured, confident answer you can practice and deliver in under 90 seconds.

---

## What to Do Next

You just created 4 career assets in 30 minutes. Here's what to tackle next based on where you are:

**If you're just starting your job search:**
- Ask Nova for "Alternative Job Titles" to expand your search
- Ask Nova to "Create a Prospective Company List" of 50 targets
- Ask Nova to "Break Down a Job Description" for a role you're interested in

**If you're actively applying:**
- Ask Nova to write a "Cover Letter" tailored to a specific job posting
- Ask Nova for "Resume Achievements" using the CAR method (Challenge, Action, Result)
- Ask Nova to "Optimize My Resume for ATS" with a specific job description

**If you have interviews coming up:**
- Ask Nova to build your "STAR Story Bank" (10-12 stories covering common themes)
- Ask Nova for "Custom Interview Questions" based on the job description
- Ask Nova to "Identify Employer Objections" so you can address concerns proactively

**If you're negotiating an offer:**
- Ask Nova for "Salary Negotiation Scripts" with word-for-word responses
- Ask Nova to "Evaluate This Job Offer" by pasting in the details
- Ask Nova to write a "Counter-Offer Response" email
